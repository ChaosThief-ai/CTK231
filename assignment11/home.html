Hello There!

I'm Willow, known online as Wispyyy (or WWWispyyy). I'm a game design student, but don't let that give you the wrong idea; I dabble in all sorts of creative areas and consider myself a jack-of-many-trades!

I consider myself fluent in music creation, sound design, image editing, vector creation, game design, and video editing. Of course, I don't consider myself to know everything about these topics——I'm always learning by doing! Things I'm still learning are programming in HTML, programming in CSS, programming in C#, storywriting and outlining, and character writing.

Since I mentioned I was programming HTML and CSS, I thought it fitting to advertise that this entire site was actually created from scratch, by me! This place is meant to showcase all information about me, from classes I've taken, to projects I'm working on! There are even pages dedicated to games I'm passionate about! If you'd like any suggestions about where you should go, there's a panel on the right with the pages I've written recently or am eager to show off.


Go ahead and check things out!
